Integrating expert knowledge into SLMs

These artefacts generalize poorly and are typically deployed in environments whose confidentiality requirements prevent the use of public-cloud LLM services.


In this context, we integrate expert knowledge into self-hosted, domain-specialized small language models (SLMs). By internalizing expert patterns into model parameters, we obtain SLMs that provide semantic reasoning and equip existing systems with a semantic layer that enhances task-specific capabilities, while remaining compatible with on-premises constraints.
